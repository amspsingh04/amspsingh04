Football analytics — templates only (nothing here is routed until you publish).

1) One reusable MDX scaffold
   - Duplicate: src/content/blogs/football/_templates/GENERAL-POST.mdx
   - To:       src/content/blogs/football/<your-slug>.mdx
   (Keep _templates/GENERAL-POST.mdx as the master copy; do not publish from _templates/.)

2) Edit the new file
   Replace placeholders with your article. Remove sections you do not need.

3) Register the post in:
   src/data/footballPosts.js
   Export an array of { id, slug, title, date, author, summary, mdxPath }.
   mdxPath must be relative to src/content/blogs/, e.g. "football/my-slug.mdx"

4) Build
   npm run build

5) Section / sorting
   - Live posts appear at /football (oldest post first, then newer).
   - /blogs stays separate (writing + PDFs only).

MDX building blocks (see GENERAL-POST.mdx): fenced code, Callout, ConsoleOutput, AnalyticsFigure, DataTable, GFM tables.
Optional: import a client chart from src/components/ at the top of your MDX (useDynamicImport is enabled).
